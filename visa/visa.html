Accepted international students will receive a letter of acceptance from the university in order to start visa processing with the Azerbaijani Embassy. For more information regarding visa issues, visit the websites below:

www.evisa.gov.az

The electronic visa issued within three working days through the "ASAN Visa" system allows a stay of up to 30 days, with a state fee of USD 20. Despite non-working days and holidays, an urgent visa issuing opportunity within 3 hours through the "ASAN Visa" system is available. Detailed information can be found at https://www.evisa.gov.az/az.

Home Page | The Electronic Visa System of Azerbaijan Republic: www.evisa.gov.az. Electronic visas are issued only to nationals of the countries listed at this link.
